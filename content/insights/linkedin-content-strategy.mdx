LinkedIn is the best platform for B2B lead generation. But most people use it wrong.

Here's the strategy that actually works.

## Why LinkedIn Matters for B2B

**LinkedIn stats**:
- 900M+ users
- 65M decision-makers
- 4 out of 5 members drive business decisions
- 2x higher conversion rate than other platforms

If you're in B2B, LinkedIn should be your #1 content platform.

## The LinkedIn Algorithm in 2024

LinkedIn prioritizes:

**1. Engagement bait**: Posts that spark conversation  
**2. Dwell time**: How long people spend reading  
**3. Shares**: Especially shares with commentary  
**4. Early engagement**: First hour is critical  
**5. Profile completeness**: Complete profiles get more reach

**What it penalizes**:
- External links (in the post)
- Hashtag spam (more than 5)
- Engagement pods
- Reposted content from other platforms

## The Content Framework

### Post Types That Work

**1. Personal Stories** (Highest engagement)
- Share failures and lessons
- Behind-the-scenes
- Career journey
- Vulnerable moments

**Example**: "I lost $50K on a failed product launch. Here's what I learned..."

**2. Contrarian Takes** (High engagement)
- Challenge common beliefs
- Go against the grain
- Spark debate

**Example**: "Unpopular opinion: Most marketing automation is making your marketing worse."

**3. How-To Guides** (High value)
- Step-by-step tutorials
- Frameworks
- Templates
- Actionable advice

**Example**: "How we generated 100 qualified leads in 30 days (exact process)"

**4. Data-Driven Insights** (High authority)
- Original research
- Case studies
- Performance data
- Industry trends

**Example**: "We analyzed 10,000 LinkedIn posts. Here's what actually works..."

**5. Curated Lists** (High saves)
- Tool recommendations
- Resource roundups
- Book lists
- Best practices

**Example**: "10 AI tools that save me 20 hours per week"

### Post Structure

**Hook** (First 2 lines):
- Grab attention
- Make them want to read more
- Show the benefit

**Body**:
- Short paragraphs (1-2 lines)
- Bullet points
- White space
- Easy to scan

**CTA**:
- Ask a question
- Request engagement
- Invite discussion

**Example**:

```
I spent $10K on LinkedIn ads before I figured this out.

Now I generate 50+ qualified leads per month organically.

Here's the exact strategy:

1. Post daily (consistency beats perfection)
2. Focus on one topic (become known for something)
3. Engage before you post (warm up the algorithm)
4. Reply to every comment (within 1 hour)
5. DM people who engage (build relationships)

The secret? It's not about going viral.

It's about building trust with the right 100 people.

What's your LinkedIn strategy?
```

## Posting Frequency

**Minimum**: 3x per week  
**Optimal**: 5x per week  
**Maximum**: 1x per day

**Best times**:
- Tuesday-Thursday
- 7-9 AM or 12-1 PM
- When your audience is active

## Content Themes

Don't post randomly. Have themes:

**Monday**: Industry insights  
**Tuesday**: How-to guides  
**Wednesday**: Personal stories  
**Thursday**: Data and research  
**Friday**: Weekend inspiration

This creates consistency and expectation.

## The Engagement Strategy

**Before you post** (30 minutes):
- Comment on 10 posts in your niche
- Reply to comments on your previous posts
- Engage with your target audience

This "warms up" the algorithm.

**After you post** (First hour is critical):
- Reply to every comment
- Ask follow-up questions
- Keep the conversation going

**Throughout the day**:
- Engage with others' content
- Build relationships
- Be genuinely helpful

## The DM Strategy

When someone engages with your content:

**Don't**: Immediately pitch your product  
**Do**: Start a genuine conversation

**Example**:

"Hey [Name], saw your comment on my post about [topic]. Really insightful point about [their comment]. 

I'm curious - how are you currently handling [related problem]?"

Build the relationship first. The business comes later.

## Common Mistakes

**Mistake 1**: Posting and ghosting  
**Fix**: Engage before and after posting.

**Mistake 2**: Only promoting your product  
**Fix**: 90% value, 10% promotion.

**Mistake 3**: Using LinkedIn like Twitter  
**Fix**: Longer, more thoughtful content works better.

**Mistake 4**: Ignoring DMs  
**Fix**: Respond within 24 hours. Build relationships.

**Mistake 5**: Inconsistent posting  
**Fix**: Post at least 3x per week. Consistency wins.
