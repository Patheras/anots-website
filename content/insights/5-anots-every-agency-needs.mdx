You just signed up for ANOTS. You have 3 free ANOTs per month (or 50 on Standard, unlimited on Pro). **Where do you start?**

Here are the 5 ANOTs that deliver immediate value. No learning curve. Just results.

## 1. Weekly Social Media Scheduler

**What it does**: Generates 7 days of social posts (Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter), schedules them automatically.

**Why it works**: Most agencies spend 2-3 hours per week on social scheduling. This ANOT does it in 10 minutes.

**How to set it up**:
- Connect your social accounts (Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter)
- Set posting frequency (1x/day, 2x/day, etc.)
- Define content themes (product updates, tips, behind-the-scenes)
- Approve the batch once per week

**Result**: 7 days of content, scheduled and published. You review once, approve once, done.

---

## 2. Client Report Generator

**What it does**: Pulls metrics from Google Analytics, social media, ad platforms. Generates a branded PDF report. Emails it to your client.

**Why it works**: Client reporting is tedious. This ANOT makes it automatic.

**How to set it up**:
- Connect data sources (GA4, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads)
- Define report template (metrics, charts, insights)
- Set schedule (weekly, monthly)
- Add client email addresses

**Result**: Clients get reports automatically. You look proactive. They stay happy.

---

## 3. Content Repurposer

**What it does**: Takes one long-form piece (blog post, video, podcast) and creates 10+ micro-content pieces (tweets, LinkedIn posts, Instagram carousels, email snippets).

**Why it works**: You already create great content. This ANOT multiplies its reach.

**How to set it up**:
- Connect content source (WordPress, YouTube, podcast RSS)
- Define output formats (tweets, LinkedIn posts, etc.)
- Set distribution channels
- Review and approve the batch

**Result**: One blog post becomes 10 social posts, 5 tweets, 3 email snippets. Maximum leverage.

---

## 4. Lead Magnet Delivery System

**What it does**: When someone fills out a form (lead magnet, ebook, checklist), this ANOT sends the download link, adds them to your CRM, starts a nurture sequence.

**Why it works**: Manual lead follow-up is slow. This ANOT responds in seconds.

**How to set it up**:
- Connect form tool (Typeform, Google Forms, Webflow)
- Connect email tool (Gmail, SendGrid, Mailchimp)
- Connect CRM (HubSpot, Pipedrive, Airtable)
- Define nurture sequence (3-5 emails over 2 weeks)

**Result**: Leads get instant value. You never miss a follow-up. Conversion rates go up.

---

## 5. Competitor Monitoring Alert

**What it does**: Monitors competitor websites, social media, and news mentions. Sends you a weekly digest of what they're doing.

**Why it works**: Staying ahead of competitors is hard. This ANOT makes it automatic.

**How to set it up**:
- Add competitor URLs and social handles
- Define keywords to track (product launches, pricing changes, campaigns)
- Set alert frequency (daily, weekly)
- Choose notification method (email, Slack, Discord)

**Result**: You always know what competitors are doing. No manual checking required.

## Bonus: The Meta-ANOT

Once you're comfortable, create an ANOT that **manages your other ANOTs**:

- Monitors performance of all running ANOTs
- Suggests optimizations (better posting times, content themes)
- Pauses underperforming ANOTs
- Reports weekly on what's working

Yes, you can automate your automation management. That's the point.
